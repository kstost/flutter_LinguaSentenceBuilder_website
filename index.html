‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play LingoPuzzle?

Read the Korean sentence at the top of the screen. Then, drag and drop English words from the word bank into the answer slots to form the correct English translation. Tap "Check Answer" when you're ready!

What are the difficulty levels?

LingoPuzzle offers three levels: Beginner (simple sentences), Intermediate (common phrases), and Advanced (complex sentence structures). Start at your comfort level and progress as you improve!

How does Text-to-Speech work?

When you complete a puzzle correctly, the app will speak the English sentence aloud so you can hear the proper pronunciation. This helps reinforce your listening and speaking skills!

What happens if I make a mistake?

No worries! If your answer is incorrect, you'll get feedback and can try again. You can rearrange the words as many times as you need until you find the right solution.

Is there a time limit?

No! Learn at your own pace. Take your time to think about each sentence and build your English skills without pressure.
